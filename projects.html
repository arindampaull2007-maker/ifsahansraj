Economic Modeling

Infinite-Sum Modeling Inc. (ISM)

A leading economic firm providing advice to government, NGOs, and companies.

We prepared an economic model to assess costs of metal weathering and threats to national infrastructure. Our team undertook cost-benefit analysis using the Hoar method, Engineering Economy method, and Present Value method, suggesting a Corrosion Management System.

In the next leg, we compiled research on climate change impacts on tourism and challenges in India's toy industry, gaining expertise in economic modeling and analogies of the Indian economy.

Economic Modeling Cost-Benefit Analysis Climate Research
